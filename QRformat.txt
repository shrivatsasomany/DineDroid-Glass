Use the following string and go to goqr.me (or any other QR code generator) to make a QR code for this app to scan.

WIFI:S:ssid;U:username;P:password;E:PEAP;PH:MS-CHAPv2;;

S - ssid
U - username
P - password
E - EAP method
PH - phase2 authentication
A - anonymous identity
CC - client certificate
PK - private key
CA - CA certificate

Note: QR code generators online may or may not send this string (containing your password) in cleartext to their servers.
